# #60 Interval Snap | Fridge Combo

A minimalist, high-speed rest timer designed for athletes, gym-goers, and yogis. When you're pushing your physical limits, you don't have time to fiddle with complex apps. Just "Snap" your rest time and get back to work.

## 🟢 One Action, Zero Friction
- **One-Tap Start:** Huge 30s, 60s, and 90s buttons. No setting, just hitting.
- **Stay Awake:** Uses the Wake Lock API to prevent your screen from dimming or locking during your set.
- **Visual Progress:** The entire background fills with a soft green bar, turning orange for the final 5 seconds. See your progress even from across the room.
- **Smart Audio Unlock:** Specifically optimized for iOS Safari to ensure the "Finish Beep" sounds even when the device is usually restricted.

## 🛠 Target Users
- **Weightlifters:** Precise inter-set rest management.
- **Yogis:** Timing static holds or transition periods.
- **HIIT Practitioners:** Fast-paced interval switching with high visibility.

## 📐 Fridge Combo Design System
- **Background:** `#fff`
- **Primary Color:** `#333`
- **Border Radius:** `15px`
- **Performance:** Lightweight, offline-ready, browser-only.
- **Privacy:** No login, No tracking, No ads. Pure focus.

## 📱 Installation (PWA)
1. Open the tool in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android).
2. Tap "Add to Home Screen".
3. Use it as a native app right on your gym floor.
